Why Use Quorum?

Diverse Perspectives

Each AI model has unique strengths, training data, and reasoning patterns. Combining them yields richer insights.

Consensus Building

Three Operational Modes

Choose the right mode for your use case

Quick Consensus

Single round ‚Äî All providers respond independently in parallel. Fast but may have lower agreement. Best for straightforward queries.

Full Deliberation

Three rounds ‚Äî Round 1: Independent analysis. Round 2: Cross-review. Round 3: Final synthesis. Best for complex decisions.

Devil's Advocate

Critical analysis ‚Äî One provider challenges assumptions and presents counterarguments. Best for validating ideas and finding weaknesses.
